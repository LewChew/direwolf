About Direwolf Petshop

Learn about our mission and our team

Our Story

Direwolf Petshop was founded in 2020 by Brandon Stark, a direwolf enthusiast with a vision to connect these magnificent creatures with loving homes.

After rescuing his first direwolf, Summer, from poachers beyond the Wall, Brandon discovered the incredible bond that can form between humans and direwolves. He established the first direwolf sanctuary in Winterfell before expanding to a full-service adoption center.

Today, Direwolf Petshop is the leading direwolf adoption center in the Seven Kingdoms, with over 100 successful adoptions and counting.

Our Mission

At Direwolf Petshop, our mission is to:

  • Rescue direwolves from dangerous situations and provide them with care
  • Match direwolves with compatible owners who will respect their wild nature
  • Educate the public about proper direwolf care and training
  • Advocate for direwolf conservation in their natural habitats

Ethical Practices

We are committed to ethical breeding and adoption practices. All our direwolves:

  • Come from rescue situations or certified ethical breeders
  • Receive full veterinary care, including vaccinations and health screenings
  • Are properly socialized and trained before adoption
  • Are matched with owners based on compatibility, not just desire
